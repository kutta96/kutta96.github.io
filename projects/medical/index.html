Innovative Medical Device

Brief Introduction

Plasma activated water (PAW) is an innovative fungicide widely studied in recent years. We designed the device model in SolidWorks, and used 3D printing to produce the first prototype. We used Arduino development board to achieve automatic control and human-machine interaction. The figure shows the picture of our specially designed electrode structure, and the purple color indicates the appearance of plasmas. In fact, this electrode structure could efficiently break down the air and induce plasmas with the power at only ~2W.

Features

  • Better bactericidal effects
  • Lower price ($30)
  • No side effects on humans
  • Home-made liquid level sensor
  • Low power
