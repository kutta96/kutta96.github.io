About

CV   Transcript   chang2468135@stu.xjtu.edu.cn

I am a research intern at State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang University, supervised by Dr. Zhong Ren. My current research interests are Monte Carlo rendering, blue-noise dithered sampling and perceptual optimization. Before that I was a research intern at China Academy of Information and Communications Technology (CAICT), supervised by Dr. Zhen Chen, focusing on real-time rendering techniques and industry survey of virtual reality and augmented reality. I was an exchange student in National University of Singapore, supervised by Dr. LOW Kok Lim.

Meanwhile, I am skilled at using C++ and MATLAB to complete projects. I am also familiar with GLSL and open-source libraries like OpenGL, Numpy, PCL and OpenCV.

I obtained my master degree and bachelor degree in Electrical Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University, Xi'an. During the graduate education, I conducted research on optical detection and laser optics under the guidance of professors Mingzhe Rong and Yi Wu.
